Safety Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to ensure your safety and well-being.

General Safety

  • Always wear protective gear when handling machinery.
  • Ensure proper ventilation in enclosed spaces.
  • Follow proper lifting techniques to avoid injuries.

Emergency Procedures

  • Know the location of all emergency exits and first aid kits.
  • In case of fire, evacuate immediately and avoid using elevators.
  • For medical emergencies, call the emergency services number provided in your area.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

  • Always wear gloves, goggles, and helmets as required for your task.
  • Ensure your PPE is in good condition and properly fitted.
  • Do not use damaged PPE and report any issues immediately.

Health and Wellness

  • Take regular breaks to avoid fatigue.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain good nutrition.
  • If you feel unwell, report to your supervisor and rest.
