Emergency Procedures

Follow these steps to stay safe during a flood.

Flood Emergency

  • Move to higher ground immediately if you are in a flood-prone area.
  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwatersâ€”just 6 inches of moving water can knock you down.
  • Turn off utilities if instructed to do so. Do not touch electrical equipment if you are wet or standing in water.
  • Listen to emergency broadcasts for updates and evacuation orders.
  • If trapped in a building, move to the highest level but avoid attics without exits.
